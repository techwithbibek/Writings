## Remove Very

| **Very + Word** | **Stronger Word**       | **Example**                                 |
| --------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Very afraid     | **Terrified**           | She was terrified when she heard the noise. |
| Very angry      | **Furious**             | He was furious about the mistake.           |
| Very bad        | **Awful / Terrible**    | The food tasted awful.                      |
| Very beautiful  | **Gorgeous / Stunning** | She looked stunning in that dress.          |
| Very big        | **Enormous / Huge**     | They live in an enormous house.             |
| Very boring     | **Tedious / Dull**      | The lecture was extremely tedious.          |
| Very bright     | **Dazzling**            | The sun was dazzling this morning.          |
| Very busy       | **Swamped**             | I'm swamped with work today.                |
| Very calm       | **Serene**              | The lake looked serene at sunset.           |
| Very careful    | **Meticulous**          | He is meticulous about his notes.           |
| Very clean      | **Spotless**            | The kitchen is spotless.                    |
| Very cold       | **Freezing**            | It’s freezing outside.                      |
| Very colorful   | **Vibrant**             | The painting is vibrant and full of life.   |
| Very confused   | **Perplexed**           | I was perplexed by the instructions.        |
| Very crowded    | **Packed**              | The train was packed in the morning.        |
| Very cute       | **Adorable**            | Your puppy is adorable!                     |
